Pilot Program

A comprehensive 12-month initiative to demonstrate climate resilience impact for smallholder farmers across Uganda

Program Overview

Our pilot program is designed to validate our climate resilience approach and demonstrate measurable impact in real farming communities

12
Months Duration
Comprehensive implementation and monitoring period
2000

Smallholder farmers across multiple regions

Program Objectives

Primary Objectives

  • Demonstrate 30% increase in crop yields through climate-smart practices
  • Reduce crop loss from climate events by 40%
  • Improve household income by 25% through better market access
  • Build sustainable farmer networks and cooperatives

Secondary Objectives

  • Validate technology platform effectiveness
  • Document best practices for scaling
  • Establish partnerships with local organizations
  • Create replicable implementation model
